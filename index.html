Frequently Asked Questions

Our fee for adopting an adult dog is £200. Every dog we rehome is vaccinated, microchipped and neutered (or we’ll give you a neutering voucher if they haven't yet been neutered).

We’re receiving more adoption applications than ever before, so please don’t be disheartened if we don’t get back to you straight away. You should receive a response from us within six days of submitting your application.

All the dogs in our care are thoroughly assessed by our behaviour experts to determine their ideal home. Some will be able to live with another dog, and some won’t. If we match you with a dog, we’ll ask you to bring your other dog to the rehoming centre for them to meet, so we can be sure that they will be happy to live together.
